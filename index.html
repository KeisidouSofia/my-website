Recycling Guide

Learn the correct ways to recycle different types of products

  • Plastic
  • Paper
  • Metal
  • Electronics
  • Glass

Plastic

Rinse containers, remove caps and labels. Separate by type if required by local recycling rules.

Paper

Recycle clean paper, cardboard, newspapers. Avoid greasy or food-stained items like pizza boxes.

Metal

Recycle aluminum cans and tin cans. Rinse to remove residue and remove labels if possible.

Electronics

Do not throw electronics in regular trash. Take old phones, batteries, and appliances to certified e-waste collection centers.

Glass

Recycle bottles and jars. Remove lids, rinse, and avoid broken glass if your program does not accept it.
